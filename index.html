The Handmaiden

Released August 2016

Musicians:

  • Jo Yeong-wook, Original Music Composer
  • Kim Tae-ri, Vocals
  • THE SOUNDTRACKINGS, Performer
  • Gain, MINSEO, Vocals & Song-writers

Park Chan-Wook’s "The Handmaiden" is a 2016 South Korean psychological thriller film set in Japanese-occupied Korea in the 1930s. This is a film made by an artist at the peak of his powers: Park, a South Korean director who started out as a critic, has many great or near-great genre films, including “Oldboy,” “Sympathy for Mr. Vengeance,” “Lady Vengeance” and “Thirst,” but this one is so intricate yet light-footed that it feels like the summation of his career to date.

Snowpiercer

Released July 2013

Musicians:

  • Marco Beltrami, Original Music Composer
  • Taeyoung Choi, Sound Editor
  • Anna Behlmer, Sound Mixer

Snowpiercer is a 2013 science-fiction action film based on the French graphic novel Le Transperceneige by Jacques Lob, Benjamin Legrand and Jean-Marc Rochette. In a future where a failed climate change experiment has killed all life except for the survivors who boarded the Snowpiercer (a train that travels around the globe), a new class system emerges.
